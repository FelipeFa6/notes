## Morning: The Slow & Steady Win

**7:00 AM**
- Naturally wake up
- No alarm clock shock
- Feel genuinely rested

**7:15 AM**
- Hydrate (large glass of water)
- Coffee or tea
- No screens
- Read or enjoy silence

**7:45 AM**
- Shower
- Get dressed
- Feel put-together
- Signal work mode begins

---

## Midday: Deep Work & a Break

*Leverage morning energy for focused work.*

**9:00 AM**
- Deep work block
- Hardest task first
- Notifications off
- Enter flow state

**11:30 AM**
- Check emails
- Attend meetings
- Catch up with team
- Feel ahead (big task done)

**12:30 PM**
- Step away from desk
- Eat lunch elsewhere
- Short walk outside
- Sunlight and fresh air
- Clear your head

---

## Afternoon: Wrapping Up & Shutting Down

*Manage energy slumps and create a clear end to the workday.*

**3:00 PM**
- Smaller tasks
- Admin work
- Creative stuff
- Lighter concentration

**5:00 PM**
- Write tomorrow's to-do list
- Get tasks out of your head
- Close all tabs
- Shut down computer
- Leave workspace
- Work is officially over

---

## Evening: Transition & Gym

*Use exercise to burn off stress and signal body to rest.*

**5:30 PM**
- Change into gym gear
- Shift from worker to athlete
- Listen to music or podcast
- Head to gym

**6:15 PM**
- Workout time
- Lift, run, or stretch
- Sweat it out
- Release work stress
- Feel strong and accomplished

**7:15 PM**
- Head home
- Take a shower
- Rehydrate
- Muscles tired (good tired)

---

## Night: The 8-Hour Prep

*Create a calm wind-down routine for easy, early sleep.*

**8:00 PM**
- Light dinner
- Sit down to eat
- With family or partner
- Comfort show (not intense)
- No work talk

**9:00 PM**
- Digital sunset
- Phone on Do Not Disturb
- Leave in another room
- Screens off

**9:15 PM**
- Low-stimulation activities:
  - Read physical book
  - Calm podcast or audiobook
  - Gentle stretching
  - Foam rolling
  - Journal one sentence

**9:45 PM**
- Brush teeth
- Wash face
- Dim the lights
- Room cool, dark, quiet
- Get into bed

**10:00 PM**
- Lights out
- Fall asleep quickly
- Feel content
- Feel productive
- Feel tired (best way)

---

## The Result

You drift off knowing:

- Present for your work
- Took care of your body
- Gifted yourself 8 hours of sleep

Tomorrow you wake up.
And do it all again.

Simple.
Powerful.
Deeply satisfying.
